Meet the team!

Our Mission

To provide you with an insight of events of your intrest

Hello, we are Lokal Events. Our mission is to create an app that can help people find events in their city, we currently are only working on the city of Fresno, CA. Our target audience is college students age 18-24 because they don't have a lot of free money yet still want to enjoy themselves, but anyone can use the app. It will be a personalized app with your own profile and you can put in your intrests and the app will do the heavy lifting. Also provide you with events that best fit you, they will be able to buy tickets and finance the tickets because we understand that money can be an issue.Lokal Events will help you grow local business as they will be able to purchase ad space on our app as well as create deals in relation with Lokal.
